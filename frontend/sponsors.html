Frequently Asked Questions

  • helpWhat audience does VelvetQuill reach?

    VelvetQuill connects with a diverse community of romance readers and writers, primarily aged 18-45, with a strong female demographic (85%). Our audience is highly engaged, spending an average of 45 minutes per session on the platform.

  • helpCan I sponsor specific content or categories?

    Yes! Our Silver and Gold sponsorship tiers allow for category-specific placements. You can align your brand with particular romance genres like historical, contemporary, fantasy, or LGBTQ+ stories.

  • helpWhat is the minimum sponsorship commitment?

    We require a 3-month minimum commitment for all sponsorship tiers to ensure meaningful engagement with our community. Longer commitments (6-12 months) receive additional benefits and discounted rates.

  • helpHow are sponsorship metrics tracked?

    We provide monthly performance reports including impressions, clicks, and engagement metrics. Gold partners receive additional insights including audience demographics and content performance data.
