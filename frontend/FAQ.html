Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VelvetQuill

How Can We Help You?

How do I publish my stories on VelvetQuill?

To publish your stories:

  1. Create a free account or log in to your existing account
  2. Navigate to your Dashboard and click "Create New Story"
  3. Fill in your story details (title, summary, tags, etc.)
  4. Add your content using our easy-to-use editor
  5. Select appropriate content warnings and ratings
  6. Preview your story and click "Publish"

Your story will be immediately available to our community of readers!

What are the benefits for writers on VelvetQuill?

Writers on VelvetQuill enjoy numerous benefits:

  • Global Exposure: Reach readers worldwide
  • Portfolio Building: Showcase your work professionally
  • Follower Base: Grow your dedicated reader community
  • Writing Contests: Participate in regular competitions with prizes
  • Reader Feedback: Receive comments and engagement on your stories
  • Community: Connect with other romance writers
Are there any restrictions on story length or format?

We welcome stories of all lengths, from flash fiction to novel-length works. You can publish:

  • Short stories (1,000-7,500 words)
  • Novellas (7,500-40,000 words)
  • Novels (40,000+ words)
  • Serialized works (published in chapters)

For longer works, we recommend publishing in chapters to keep readers engaged. There are no specific formatting requirements beyond using our built-in editor or pasting properly formatted text.

How do writing contests work?

VelvetQuill hosts regular writing contests with different themes, genres, and requirements. Here's how they work:

  1. Contests are announced on our Contests page and through community notifications
  2. Each contest has specific guidelines, word counts, and deadlines
  3. You submit your entry through the contest submission form
  4. Entries are judged by our panel or through community voting
  5. Winners receive prizes, recognition, and featured placement on the platform

Contest participation is free for all registered members.

How do I find stories I'll enjoy?

There are several ways to discover great stories on VelvetQuill:

  • Browse by Category: Filter by romance sub-genres like contemporary, historical, paranormal, etc.
  • Use Tags: Search for specific tropes or themes using our tag system
  • Collections: Explore curated collections by our editors
  • Follow Authors: Follow your favorite writers to see their new works
  • Recommendations: Get personalized recommendations based on your reading history
  • Community Lists: Check out reading lists created by other members
Can I interact with writers on the platform?

Absolutely! We encourage reader-writer interaction through:

  • Comments: Leave comments on stories you enjoy
  • Direct Messages: Send private messages to writers (if they have this feature enabled)
  • Reviews: Provide constructive feedback on stories
  • Community Forums: Participate in discussions with writers and other readers
  • Following: Follow your favorite writers to get updates on their new works

We ask that all interactions remain respectful and constructive.

Is there a mobile app for reading?

Currently, VelvetQuill is a web-based platform that is fully optimized for mobile browsers. You can access all features through your phone's browser with the same functionality as the desktop version.

We are developing a dedicated mobile app, which will be released in the future. In the meantime, you can add our website to your home screen for an app-like experience:

  • On iOS: Use the "Share" button and select "Add to Home Screen"
  • On Android: Use the menu button and select "Add to Home Screen"
Is VelvetQuill free to use?

Yes! VelvetQuill is completely free for both readers and writers. There are no subscription fees, reading fees, or publishing fees.

We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core platform will always remain free to use.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 15 minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. However, please note:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Your published stories will be removed from the platform
  • Your comments and interactions will be anonymized

To delete your account:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Scroll to the "Account Management" section
  3. Click "Delete Account" and confirm your choice
What content is not allowed on VelvetQuill?

To maintain a safe and inclusive community, we prohibit:

  • Sexually explicit depictions involving minors (under 18 years)
  • Racism and racist content
  • Hate speech targeting protected characteristics
  • Plagiarism or copyright infringement
  • Spam, scams, or malicious content

Beyond these restrictions, we encourage creative freedom across all romance sub-genres. Please review our complete Content Guidelines for detailed information.

How do content warnings work?

We use a content warning system to help readers make informed choices:

  • When publishing, writers must select appropriate content warnings
  • Common warnings include: explicit sexual content, violence, abuse, self-harm, etc.
  • Stories with certain warnings are filtered and tagged appropriately
  • Readers can filter out content with specific warnings if desired

This system ensures that readers can find content that matches their preferences while allowing writers creative freedom.

Who owns the copyright to my stories?

You retain full copyright to all original stories you publish on VelvetQuill. By posting your work here, you grant VelvetQuill a license to display and distribute your content on our platform, but you maintain all ownership rights.

You are free to remove your stories at any time, and you can publish your work elsewhere (unless you've entered into exclusive publishing agreements through contests or other programs).

For complete details, please see our Terms of Use.
